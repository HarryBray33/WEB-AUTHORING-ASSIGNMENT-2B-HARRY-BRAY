About
The
Website

This website is an archive dedicated to unreleased music, forgotten recordings, and rare material. Users can explore trending tracks, artist histories, and curated collections that celebrate music culture. Also find a variety of songs that were not released on more mainstream platforms. The goal of this website is to promote these old artists that were either forgetten or have never had the chance to be heard by a wider audience. Or perhaps due to budget or time constraints, they were never able to release their music. I hope this website can be a place for people to discover new music genre and artists that they may have never heard of before, and to promote the music that these artists have created. This website will cover all types of genres from experimental to heavy and even heavy death metal...
