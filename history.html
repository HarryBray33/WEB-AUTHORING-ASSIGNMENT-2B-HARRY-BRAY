2012

THRAILKILL, is a progressive band from Los Angeles. All members studied at the Musicians Institute in Hollywood, CA. Nothing is played to showcase something. It's all done for the sake of the music and the songs consist of careful choosen themes and developments. Intensely explorative and ground-breaking progressive music. A fusion of various genre non-specific textures and each song a unique emotional journey.
